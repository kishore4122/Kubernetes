LENS
****
1. Lens is a GUI to manage your Kubernetes clusters 
2. Multi-cluster management
3. Standalone application 
4. Real-time monitoring
5. Resource utilization charts and trends
6. Terminal access to nodes and containers
7. Use kubeconfig files to connect to the clusters 
8. Helm charts management
9. 1-click objects deployment through YAML templates
10. Available for Linux, macOS, and Windows

https://k8slens.dev/
